11 Footer

The footer is a section at the bottom of a website that typically contains information about the website, such as contact information, hours of operation, and social media links. It can also include a form for users to sign up for a newsletter or other updates. The footer is an important part of a website because it provides users with additional information and helps them navigate the site. In the redesign for the Outback Nursery website, the footer includes contact information, hours of operation, a map showing the location of the nursery, and a form for users to sign up for a newsletter.
