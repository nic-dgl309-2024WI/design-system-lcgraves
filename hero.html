09 Hero

The hero section is a large section at the top of a page that is used to display a title, image, and call-to-action. It is the first thing a user sees when they visit a website. It is used to grab the user's attention and encourage them to take action. The Outback Nursery hero section displays a large image of the nursery grounds with a title and two call-to-action buttons. The hero section is a great way to introduce your website and make a strong first impression.

Hero example

We love plants and trees as much as you do.
