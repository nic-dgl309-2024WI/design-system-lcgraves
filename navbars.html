07 Navbars

Navbars are a key part of any website. They provide a way for users to navigate around the site and access different pages. Navbars can be horizontal or vertical, and can be fixed to the top or bottom of the page. The Outback Nursery navbar is also responsive for desktop and mobile.

On desktop, the navbar is fixed to the top of the page and includes a dropdown for the Plants section. On mobile, the navbar is hidden by default and can be accessed by clicking the hamburger icon in the top right corner of the page.
