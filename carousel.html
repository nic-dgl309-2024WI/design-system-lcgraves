10 Carousel and Pagination

Carousels are a great way to showcase multiple images or pieces of content in a small space. They are often used on homepages to display a range of products or services. Carousels can be set to automatically scroll through the content or can be controlled by the user. Pagination is used to show the user how many slides there are in the carousel and which slide they are currently viewing.

Carousel and Pagination example

We've trusted Outback Nursery for eight years, drawn by its unparalleled selection of Japanese maples and foundation trees. It's a three-generation, family-owned gem where passion for gardening is palpable. From walking through their greenhouses to receiving tailored advice and healthy plants, Outback has become a cornerstone of our garden's success. Plus, their fair prices are unbeatable.
