10 Carousel and Pagination

Carousels are a great way to showcase multiple images or pieces of content in a small space. They are often used on homepages to display a range of products or services. Carousels can be set to automatically scroll through the content or can be controlled by the user. Pagination is used to show the user how many slides there are in the carousel and which slide they are currently viewing.

Carousel and Pagination example

Don and Michael

We've trusted Outback Nursery for eight years, drawn by its unparalleled selection of Japanese maples and foundation trees. It's a three-generation, family-owned gem where passion for gardening is palpable. From walking through their greenhouses to receiving tailored advice and healthy plants, Outback has become a cornerstone of our garden's success. Plus, their fair prices are unbeatable.

Richard Manton

I own a small landscaping business and Outback Nursery is my go to for all my plant & garden needs and they're incredibly helpful. I would recommend Outback to anyone!

Emma Peterson

Relocating to Ships Point introduced us to Outback Nursery, and it's been a game-changer. The family's depth of knowledge, combined with their friendly service and excellent pricing, has helped transform our acreage into a thriving garden.

Ivy Blooms Home & Garden Inc

Outback Nursery isn't just a place to buy plants; it's a haven for garden enthusiasts. With an outstanding variety of plants, passionate staff, and excellent value, shopping here is a delightful experience. It's the perfect place to nurture your gardening passion.

Karen Latiff

Recommended by a friend, Outback Nursery exceeded our expectations. Their vast selection, coupled with the team's readiness to assist, made our garden project enjoyable. Their support in planning a water feature shows they go above and beyond.
