08 Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs are a secondary navigation aid that help users understand where they are in relation to the rest of the site. They are typically displayed horizontally and provide links to each previous page the user navigated through to get to the current page. Breadcrumbs are especially useful for sites with deep hierarchies or multiple levels of content.

Breadcrumbs example

  1. Home >
  2. Plants >
  3. Trees >
  4. Fruit Trees
